Introduction

Welcome to our comprehensive Blinkit analysis. Using data from March 2023 to November 2024, we have developed interactive Tableau visualizations that capture key trends—from sales and payment methods to customer behavior, delivery efficiency, inventory management, and marketing performance. Each section presents a visualization followed by detailed observations, insights, and actionable recommendations.

Sales & Payment Analysis

Overall Sales Trend Over Time (2023 & 2024)

  • Observations: Monthly data reveals that Card transactions peaked in mid-2023 (~193K) before declining in 2024 (~175K). Cash transactions surged in April 2023 (~165K) before dropping sharply (~24K in November). UPI and Wallet methods initially peaked (UPI ~183K; Wallet ~160K) with modest declines thereafter.
  • Insights: Card payments, while stable, decreased later—hinting at a shift toward digital alternatives. The significant drop in cash usage reinforces the digital payment trend, while UPI and Wallet growth is inconsistent.
  • Recommendations: Investigate the factors behind the cash decline and enhance promotions for UPI and Wallet. Also, analyze potential causes for the card payment dip, such as transaction fees or competition.

Sales by Payment Method

  • Observations: Sales rank as follows—Card (~2.86M), Cash (~2.77M), Wallet (~2.71M), UPI (~2.65M).
  • Insights: Despite the decline in card transactions, they remain dominant due to strong consumer trust. Meanwhile, digital payment methods are gradually gaining ground.
  • Recommendations: Consider targeted cashback or reward programs for digital payments and streamline the card transaction process.

Customer Insights & Segmentation

Customer Lifetime Value & Segmentation

  • Observations: Analysis shows that Premium customers have high orders and value; New customers display a wide spending range with fewer repeat orders; Regular customers offer balanced performance; and Inactive customers show limited engagement.
  • Insights: Premium customers drive revenue, while New and Inactive segments require targeted engagement strategies.
  • Recommendations: Retain premium customers with loyalty programs, offer targeted incentives for new users, and re-engage inactive users through personalized campaigns.

Customer Distribution by Location

  • Observations: Customers span India, Bangladesh, Nepal, and Sri Lanka. New customers are concentrated in metropolitan areas; Premium customers in select urban centers; and Regular customers are widely distributed with a higher density in tier‑2 cities.
  • Insights: Urban regions are key for digital adoption, while the widespread distribution of inactive users suggests the need for localized strategies.
  • Recommendations: Implement location-based promotions and tailor campaigns according to regional dynamics.

Delivery & Operational Performance

Delivery Time Deviations & Average Delivery Times

  • Observations: Although most orders are delivered on time, many experience minor delays (5–10 minutes) and a few see significant delays (15+ minutes). Average delivery times range from 3.9 to 5.3 minutes, with a slight increase toward the end of the period.
  • Insights: High order volumes during peak periods can strain logistics, where even minor delays affect customer satisfaction.
  • Recommendations: Identify operational bottlenecks, optimize delivery routes, and consider AI-based predictive analytics to manage peak demand.

Inventory & Product Performance

Damaged Stock & Top-Selling Products

  • Observations: Perishable categories (e.g., Dairy, Fruits & Vegetables, Snacks) exhibit higher damage rates, while Baby and Personal Care have lower damage. Top-selling products include pet treats, toilet cleaner, dish soap, and vitamins.
  • Insights: The vulnerability of perishables indicates potential packaging or supply chain issues. High-selling and high-margin products offer significant growth opportunities.
  • Recommendations: Enhance cold storage and packaging protocols; maintain a robust inventory of high-performing products; and consider bundling strategies to maximize profitability.

Marketing Campaign & Customer Feedback

Campaign Performance & Feedback Analysis

  • Observations (Campaign): The scatter plot of advertising spend versus revenue shows a positive trend, though some campaigns (red dots) underperform. Premium segments yield higher ROAS compared to Inactive and New users.
  • Observations (Feedback): Bar charts reveal polarized customer feedback—with many 3-star and 5-star ratings—and indicate that customer service receives more critical reviews.
  • Observations (Sentiment): Overall, positive sentiment is increasing while negative sentiment declines slightly, though customer service remains a concern.
  • Recommendations: Reallocate ad spend toward New and Premium segments, optimize underperforming campaigns, and address customer service issues by refining the app experience and responding to critical feedback.

Final Strategic Recommendations & Key Takeaways

  1. Campaign Optimization:
    • Reduce spending on low-performing campaigns (especially for Inactive Users) and reallocate budgets toward New and Premium segments.
    • Enhance landing pages and call-to-action messaging to improve conversion rates.
  2. Customer Engagement & Service:
    • Retain premium customers through exclusive benefits and loyalty programs.
    • Implement onboarding and retention strategies for New customers, and targeted re-engagement initiatives for Inactive users.
    • Address customer service challenges by streamlining response processes and enhancing overall user experience.
  3. Delivery & Operational Efficiency:
    • Identify and resolve operational bottlenecks causing minor delays using predictive analytics and route optimization.
    • Enhance real-time tracking to sustain high delivery standards.
  4. Inventory & Product Strategy:
    • Improve storage and packaging protocols for perishables to reduce damage rates.
    • Ensure a consistent supply of high-performing and high-margin products; consider bundling strategies for added profitability.
  5. Marketing & Pricing:
    • Monitor and adjust pricing strategies for lower-margin categories while leveraging high-margin segments.
    • Conduct A/B tests on ad creatives and remarketing campaigns to further improve conversion rates.

Digital payments continue to rise while cash usage declines. These insights and recommendations underscore the need for a dynamic, data-driven strategy to drive future growth and operational excellence.
